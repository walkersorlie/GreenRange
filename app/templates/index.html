About

My name is Walker Sorlie, and I am the owner and operator of Green Range, LLC! Born and raised here in Bend, Oregon, I have a deep-seated love of everything Bend has to offer, and as Bend continues to grow, I want to continue to provide sustainable and economically-accessible products for our food service industry.

Green Range, LLC (formerly Lave Edge, LLC) has operated for 14 years here in Central Oregon, so I have long-lasting connections to local food establishments.

What Do I Do?

I manage the entire lifecycle of your inventory and ordering needs! I purchase the products you want, store them in my own personal storage space, and I deliver them according to your schedule and needs.

Research Ordering Receiving Storage Delivery Problem Resolving
I keep up-to-date on the latest compostable products and always take the time to find you the best price
I make purchase orders and interact with suppliers and distributors so you don't have to
I receive products shipments and check that everything arrived in good condition.
I warehouse and store all of the products so you don't have to try and find space to fit that fifth box of cups
I deliver the products you desire on your schedule. So whenever you make an order, I can deliver that day, the next day, or whenever you'd like
I resolve all problems that may arise in this entire warehouseing lifecycle:
  • Damaged product: Handle getting replacements
  • Incorrect items sent: Handle getting correct items shipped
  • Items out of stock: Find suitable interim replacement items
